As Santa moves around the grid according to the given directions (north, south, east, west), his position on the grid changes. Each movement in a particular direction (e.g., one step to the north) results in a change of Santa‚Äôs coordinates on the grid.

  • Moving one step to the north increases Santa‚Äôs y-coordinate by 1.

So if:

  • If Santa moves north from (0, 0), he reaches (0, 1).
  • If Santa moves east from (0, 0), he reaches (1, 0).
  • If Santa moves south from (1, 0), he returns to (0, 0).

  • Santa starts at (0,0) and moves north, delivering a present to (0,1).
